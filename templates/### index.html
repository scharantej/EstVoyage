About Estonia

Estonia is a small country located on the eastern coast of the Baltic Sea. It shares borders with Latvia to the south and Russia to the east. Estonia is a member of the European Union and the Schengen Area.

Estonia is a land of contrasts, with a rich history and a modern outlook. The country is home to ancient castles and medieval cities, as well as modern skyscrapers and shopping malls. Estonia is also known for its beautiful natural scenery, including forests, lakes, and beaches.

Why Visit Estonia?

  • Medieval Charm: Estonia is home to some of the best-preserved medieval cities in Europe, including Tallinn, Tartu, and Pärnu.
  • Natural Beauty: Estonia is blessed with stunning natural scenery, including forests, lakes, and beaches. The country is also home to several national parks, including Lahemaa National Park and Soomaa National Park.
  • Rich Culture: Estonia has a rich culture that is influenced by its history, geography, and people. The country is home to a number of museums, art galleries, and theaters.
  • Delicious Cuisine: Estonian cuisine is a unique blend of traditional and modern flavors. Some of the most popular dishes include kama, mulgikapsas, and kringle.
  • Affordable Prices: Estonia is a relatively affordable country to visit. Prices for food, accommodation, and transportation are all reasonable.
